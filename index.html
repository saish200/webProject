Interesting facts about chameleon

  • There are around 160 species of chameleon.
  • Chameleons live in warm varied habitats from rainforests through to deserts.
  • The chameleons' eyes can rotate and focus separately on 180-degree arcs, so they can see two different objects at the same time.
  • This gives them a full 360-degree field of vision.

Interesting facts about cicada

  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  • They loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus.
  • Their short adult life is not unusual for bugs.

Interesting facts about panda

Interesting facts about pigeon

Interesting facts about teddy

  • Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902.
  • The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906.
